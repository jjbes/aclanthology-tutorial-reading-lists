Reading List
   
Doshi-Velez and Kim (2017) provide a great overview and motivation for interpretability research. 
Lipton (2018) and Jain and Wallace (2019) discuss some of the challenges of defining and evaluating interpretability. 
Jia and Liang (2017) help demonstrate the fragility of NLP models. 
LIME (Ribeiro et al., 2016) and saliency maps (Simonyan et al., 2014) are now standard interpretations. 
Wallace et al. (2019b) provides example NLP interpretations (interested readers can inspect their code).
