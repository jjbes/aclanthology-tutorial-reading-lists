Reading List

In addition to the papers mentioned in this proposal, a comprehensive bibliography can be found in the following website: https://boknilev.github.io/nlp-analysis-methods/.

For trainees interested in reading important studies before the tutorial, we recommend the following: 

Belinkov and Glass (2019);
Hupkes et al. (2018); 
Tenney et al. (2019b); 
Linzen et al. (2016b); 
Ettinger et al. (2018); 
Bahdanau et al. (2014); 
Li et al. (2016); 
Sennrich (2017); 
Papernot et al. (2016a); 
Ebrahimi et al. (2018).
