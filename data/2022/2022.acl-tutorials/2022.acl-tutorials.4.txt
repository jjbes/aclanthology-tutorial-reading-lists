Reading List

Please see the citations in Section 1. For participants interested in reading important studies before this tutorial, we recommend the following basic papers: 
(1) the typical AR model (Transformer) (Vaswani et al., 2017);
(2) the vanilla NAR model (Gu et al., 2018); 
(3) the typical iteration based NAR model (Ghazvininejad et al., 2019); 
(4) a study on NAR models for both text and speech tasks (Ren et al., 2020).
