Reading list

We aim to make the tutorial self-contained, but it will be helpful if the attendees can get some basic understanding of this field by going through the following reading list: 

PropBank: (Palmer et al.,2005), 
AMR: (Banarescu et al., 2013), 
UMR: (Gysel et al., 2021), 
SRL models: (Pradhan et al., 2005; He et al., 2017), 
and AMR models: (Flanigan et al., 2014; Lyu and Titov, 2018; Xu et al., 2020).
