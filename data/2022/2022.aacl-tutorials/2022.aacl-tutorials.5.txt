Reading List 

We recommend the literature cited in this paper, particularly: 

the rising threats of neural fake news (Zellers et al., 2019; Chawla, 2019), 
knowledge-driven misinformation detection (Hu et al., 2021; Fung et al., 2021; Guo et al., 2022),
intent characterization (Buchanan, 2020; Da et al., 2021), 
and study of fake news impact from a psychological point of view (Ecker et al., 2022).
