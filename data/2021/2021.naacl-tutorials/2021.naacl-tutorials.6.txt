Reading List

We offer an optional reading list for the tutorial attendees. These references allow one to understand crowdsourcing annotation basics for maximizing the learning outcomes from our hands-on tutorial.
We will nevertheless cover these materials during the workshop.

Quality Control. Dawid and Skene (1979); Li and Fukumoto (2019)
Task Design for NLP. Bernstein et al. (2010); Callison-Burch and Dredze (2010); Biemann (2013)
Incentives. Snow et al. (2008); Wang et al. (2013)
